DIY ROBOT FIGHT CLUB

Presented at Write/Speak/Code Conference, Palace of Fine Arts San Francisco Aug 16-18 2019

Overview

DIY Robot Fight Club was developed and presented by Elgin-Skye McLaren at Write/Speak/Code, an annual conference to promote the visibility and leadership of technologists of marginalized genders through peer-led professional development. Over 400 individuals were in attendence at Write/Speak/Code 2019.

Write/Speak/Code is a multiple track conference, and approximately 65 individuals actively participated in robot-making for the DIY Robot Fight Club. The competition overlapped with the conference's scheduled coffee break, and many attendees from other tracks came by to watch the final competition take place (with as many as 100 people watching at its' peak).

Organizers noted that DIY Robot Fight Club was listed as many participants' favourite activity of the conference based on feedback forms collected. Social media outreach was exceptionally positive, and Elgin was asked to run the workshop again in 2020.
